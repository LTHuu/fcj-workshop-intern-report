**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [Introduction to AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [AWS account management, budget setup, and VPC fundamentals](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Working with EC2, SSH connection, and S3 for storage](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Working with Docker, ECS/ECR, and deploying containers to AWS](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [System design, project topic selection, and Terraform basics](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Deploying system using Terraform, ECS, and integrating Cognito](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Module integration and system demonstration](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Bug fixing, system completion, and report writing](1.8-week8/)
